Here are some facts about the FF series:

  • There's a charater named Cid in every game except the first one
  • Final Fantasy X-2 was the first sequel to the main FF series, released in 2003, two years after the release of Final Fantasy X
  • Final Fantasy was originally named “Fighting Fantasy”. The title was changed to Final as this was meant to be the studio's last project. The tide changed though as this title saved Squaresoft from bankrupcy
