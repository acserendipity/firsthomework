What is Final Fantasy?

This series' was created by Hironobu Sakaguchi and is owned and developed by Square Enix (formerly Square and Squaresoft). The first entry was released in 1987 for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) This series is Square Enix's main product having seen many new entries, sequels and spin-offs through the years. The latest release is the spin-off titled "Stranger of Paradise:Final Fantasy Origin, and on the main series, Final Fantasy XVI is scheduled for release in 2023 for the new generation consoles.
